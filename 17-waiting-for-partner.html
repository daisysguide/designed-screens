Behavior notes
Push notification auto-fires to partner the moment this screen loads. Poke button is a second, manual nudge.
Poke limited to once per hour per topic to avoid spamming the partner.
When partner completes their answers, the app sends a push to the user and navigates them to Screen 18 — even if they've left this screen and gone to Home.
"Back to home" → Screen 12 Home. User will receive a push notification when partner is done.
No bottom nav on this screen.
