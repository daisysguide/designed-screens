Behavior notes
Greeting updates by time of day: "Good morning" / "Good afternoon" / "Good evening".
Badge card only appears when a new badge is earned — one per session. Tap to dismiss.
"Up next" always shows the single highest-priority topic — "Your turn" topics first, then available.
If no partner linked, Partner section shows nudge with "Invite your partner" button instead of avatar row.
Progress stats are read-only here — tap "See full progress →" for the full Progress tab.
Tapping the active tab (Home) scrolls back to top.
