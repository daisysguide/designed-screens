Key settings notes
Nudge frequency is the most important setting for retention — it's first in the list for a reason.
Guided timeline has an explainer subtext — "When on, we sequence topics based on your due date. Turn off to work through them at your own pace in any order." The subtext is essential because the toggle isn't self-explanatory.
Phone number shown instead of password — consistent with the passwordless auth model throughout the app.
Log out uses status-error (pink) label, not red — stays on brand even for destructive actions.
The two toggles (Funny push notifications, Guided timeline) are interactive — tap them in the preview.
