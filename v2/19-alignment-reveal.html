The signature product moment. Reveal animation plays before answers appear — boxes fade + scale in together (200ms ease-out). "Continue →" → Screen 19 Post-Reveal Reflection. No bottom nav.
Three alignment states
🎉 Fully aligned
Both answers identical or near-identical. Confetti animation plays. Green soft fill pill.
😌 Mostly aligned
Answers compatible but not identical. No animation. Yellow soft fill pill.
🤔 Worth a conversation
Answers diverge meaningfully. No animation. Orange soft fill pill.
Alignment pills always include their emoji — it's load-bearing for quick scanning. Sentence case only — never "FULLY ALIGNED".
