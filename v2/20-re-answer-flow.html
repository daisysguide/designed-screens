Behavior notes
Either partner can initiate re-answering. The other is notified immediately when "Yes, let's re-answer" is tapped.
Previous answers are preserved as a "before" snapshot — shown in a collapsed section on the new reveal screen.
After both re-answer, the reveal (Screen 18) plays again and post-reveal reflection (Screen 19) resets.
"Nevermind" returns to Screen 21 Completed Topic with no changes.
No bottom nav on this screen.
