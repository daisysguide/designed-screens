Behavior notes
Single price, single CTA. No tiers, no free trial, no "maybe later" in V1.
Topic count ([n]) in heading and body is dynamic — matches the user's personalized plan.
Promo code expands inline on tap — no sheet or modal. Collapses if user taps away without applying.
Terms of Service and Privacy Policy links open daisysguide.com/terms and daisysguide.com/privacy in the in-app browser — same URLs as Settings → Legal.
Restore purchase: disables link and shows spinner while restorePurchase() runs. Success shows green banner and navigates to Home after 2s. Failure shows red banner with support link. Link re-enables on failure so user can retry.
Successful purchase → Screen 09 Partner Linking. No bottom nav on this screen.
