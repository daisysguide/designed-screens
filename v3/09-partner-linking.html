Behavior notes
"Share invite link" triggers the native OS share sheet — iOS share sheet / Android share intent.
"Send via text" opens SMS app with pre-filled message including the invite link.
"Send via email" opens default mail client with pre-filled subject and body.
Tapping the URL in the invite card selects it for copying — it does not navigate.
Skip goes to Screen 06, same as sharing. The partner can still be linked later from Settings.
