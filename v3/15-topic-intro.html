Key structural decision: both paths are equally prominent. No visual hierarchy implying one is required first. Users who've read the book skip straight to answering. New users may want the guide first. The article is also accessible mid-question via a persistent link. No bottom nav.

Behavior notes
No bottom nav — user is inside a topic flow.
Back button always returns to Topic List. No "confirm exit" dialog in V1.
The article (Screen 16) is also accessible mid-question via a persistent "Read guide" link — so users aren't forced to commit to an order.
Partner status line is always visible above the CTAs — gives context before committing to answer.
Layout fits without scrolling on most devices. CTA region floats to bottom via spacer — not sticky.
