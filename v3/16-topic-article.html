The Basics
What will baby's last name be?
🌼
Dr. Daisy, MD
Medically reviewed
Wait, we have options?
A last name is part of your family's identity and often reflects your heritage. Different cultures have their own traditions — and a growing number of couples are exploring beyond the default.
Paternal last name
Provides continuity and connection to the father's lineage, but doesn't include the mother's family history in the child's name.
Combined last name
Hyphenated or blended — connects both family histories. Use it consistently across legal documents to avoid friction down the line.
Communicating openly
Start with an honest discussion. Consider cultural expectations, the significance of tradition, and how family on both sides may feel about your choice.
