The signature product moment. Screen is scrollable — staggered animation reveals each section in sequence. "Continue →" always visible at bottom. Back returns to Topics (Screen 13). No bottom nav.
Layout — multi-question scroll
StructureScrollable content area · sticky "Continue →" CTA always visible at bottom with gradient fade above it
Per-question sectionQuestion label (12px DM Sans medium, text-tertiary) · 10px below · then type-specific reveal pair · 20px top margin on sections after the first
Section gap20px from bottom of one reveal pair to top of next question label · no dividers
Note sectionConditional — shown only if ≥1 partner wrote a note · 20px margin above · "YOUR NOTES" section label above the pair
AI Summary20px below last section (note or last question)
Alignment pillCentered · 16px below summary card · one pill for the whole topic
Completed TopicScreen 22 shows this content statically — no animation on returning visits
Three alignment states
🎉 Fully aligned
Both partners' answers identical or near-identical across all questions. Confetti animation. Green pill.
😌 Mostly aligned
Answers compatible but not identical. No animation. Yellow pill.
🤔 Worth a conversation
Answers diverge meaningfully on ≥1 question. No animation. Orange pill.
Alignment is evaluated across all questions — not per question. One pill summarizes the whole topic. Emoji is load-bearing — sentence case only.
