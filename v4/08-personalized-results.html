Behavior notes
Topic count (28) and filtered count (23) are both dynamic — calculated from quiz answers. Example numbers shown here.
For returning unpaid users, this screen renders without re-running the build animation. The plan is loaded from their saved state and shown immediately. The CTA "Review and unlock your plan →" leads to Manage Topics → Paywall as normal.
Pace estimate (~10wk) is calculated from topic count and nudge frequency answer from quiz.
Col 3 of the stats row varies based on Q8 (Guided timeline vs Self-directed). Self-directed users see "Up to you / TIMING" since the pace estimate doesn't apply to their mode.
All 7 categories always shown — this screen shows the full plan, not a subset.
'Review and unlock your plan →' → Screen 15 Manage Topics (onboarding context). No bottom nav. No back button.
Screen 15 'Unlock my plan →' → Screen 09 Paywall. The Paywall is not reached directly from Screen 08.
