Conversation Preview
RoleThe hero element. Display-only teaser — not interactive. No chevrons, status pills, state dots, hover/press, or tap target. Must read as a preview, never as the tappable Topic Row.
Containersurface-card · radius-2xl · shadow-lg · rows separated by 1px hair hairlines
Question text15px DM Sans medium · text-primary · worries reframed as conversation questions
LabelsAll section labels share one style: label-sm uppercase, purple-500 — identical across both states. Personalization is signaled by dot color + wording, not label color: purple dots = from the user's Q8 answers, neutral dots = curated / universal.
Personalized variantLabel "Starting with what you told us" (purple dots) over the user's Q8 worries, then "And a few couples don't see coming" (neutral dots) over curated topics. Padded to ~4 rows.
Fallback variantSingle label "A few you'll want to sit down for" + the fixed four topics (neutral dots). Shown when Q8 is skipped / empty.
Recognition, not bespoke curation — the curated additions are explicitly labelled as not from the user's input, so we never imply every shown topic was chosen for them.
Personalization logic
SourceQ8 "What are you nervous about?" When answered, the user's worries lead (reframed as questions, labelled as theirs) and are padded to ~4 with curated universal topics. When skipped / empty, fall back to the fixed four.
"Who will handle what"→ How will we share parental and household duties?
"Making time for ourselves"→ How will we make time for ourselves as a couple?
"Deciding the right way to parent"→ What will we keep from how we were raised, and what will we change?
"How we'll get enough sleep"→ a sleep conversation topic
Due-date pill
StyleSubtle neutral chip · radius-full · rgba(26,26,26,0.04) bg · DM Sans 12.5px · text-secondary · no shadow · sits just above the CTA
Copy"~N weeks until baby's here" · N dynamic
Shown whenA due date exists and weeks-remaining ≥ 5 (threshold tunable). Scopes it to the "currently pregnant" and "expecting again" branches.
Hidden whenTrying / thinking about trying (no date), or countdown < 5 weeks — a low number reads as "not enough time" and hurts conversion.
Out of scope"Baby is already here" — that would be an age, not a countdown; a separate element, not this pill.

Behavior notes
Conversation Preview is personalized from Q8 when answered, otherwise falls back to the fixed four. Example content shown here.
Scope-line numbers are dynamic: "Plus N more" where N = plan total − topics shown; the area count is always 7.
Due-date pill renders only when a due date exists and weeks-remaining ≥ 5; hidden for the trying / thinking branch and when the countdown is too short.
"Let's begin →" → Screen 09 Paywall. No bottom nav. No back button.
"How it works" has been removed from this screen. Its content is preserved (Info Card pattern in the design system) and is being relocated to a more actionable spot (intro / just-in-time before the first question) as a separate task — not deleted from the product.
For returning unpaid users, this screen loads from saved plan state and skips the build animation; the same "Let's begin →" → Paywall path applies.
