Behavior notes
Price-first hierarchy: Headline → Price card → Sample reveal → Trust block → Footer → Sticky CTA bar. The buy action is always visible from page load; everything else cascades in.
$49 anchored against $60/year. Animated strike-through on $60/year, then $49 builds in.
Topic count is 45 wherever it appears (51 topics in the library minus 6 filtered out by quiz answers).
Brand voice: calm, considered, warm. No salesy phrasing ("only $X", "limited time offer"), no em dashes, sentence case throughout (small-caps labels via CSS text-transform: uppercase).
Successful purchase → Screen 10 Partner Linking. No bottom nav, no back button.
Production notes
Animations here use timed delays for the demo. In production the sample reveal, trust block, and footer should fire on IntersectionObserver triggers as each card enters the viewport. The price card animation fires on page load. The CTA bar has no entrance animation.
The Reddit chip uses Simple Icons' SVG of the Reddit Snoo. Before ship, verify usage aligns with Reddit's brand guidelines (source attribution is generally fine with the official SVG, clear space, no modification).
The "no questions" claim in the guarantee assumes a frictionless refund. Confirm against the actual App Store Connect refund flow before shipping; if refunds require any back-and-forth, soften the wording to "no hassle" or similar.
The "trusted by thousands of parents-to-be" aggregate is supported by approximately 4,250 unit sales. If a real review count is preferred, judge.me shows 5.0 from 56 reviews; the sales-based "thousands" framing is honest and stronger.
