Key settings notes
Nudge frequency is the most important setting for retention — it's first in the list for a reason.
Guided timeline has an explainer subtext — "When on, we sequence topics based on your due date." The date row is now always shown (when Q1 applies) regardless of toggle state. The toggle controls sequencing logic only.
Name appears at the top of Account — it's the most personal and frequently referenced field. Tapping pushes a lightweight edit screen.
Phone number shown instead of password — consistent with the passwordless auth model throughout the app.
Restore purchase is purple (positive action) — not destructive. Placed between phone number and Delete account so it's easy to find but clearly separated from destructive actions.
Log out uses status-error-text (pink) label, not red — stays on brand even for destructive actions.
Delete account is placed above Log Out — it is more permanent and warrants its own bottom sheet confirmation before anything happens.
The Account Deletion bottom sheet is interactive in the preview — tap "Delete account" to open it. Swipe down, tap Cancel, or tap the overlay to dismiss.
The two toggles (Funny push notifications, Guided timeline) are interactive — tap them in the preview.
The Q1 tab above the phone controls the Pacing date row: label, value, and visibility all change. This reflects data set during onboarding — users cannot change their Q1 answer from Settings directly.
Name row: tapping opens an edit screen with a single pre-filled text input and 'Save' primary CTA. On save, name updates in profile and user returns to Settings.
Due date / Baby's birthday row is context-sensitive based on the user's Q1 onboarding answer. It does not depend on the Guided timeline toggle state.
Q1 update ("I'm in a different stage now") triggers re-filter of topic plan. Topics that became eligible appear; topics that became ineligible move to the hidden state (recoverable via Manage Topics). Prediction Cards eligibility is also recomputed (see Screen 24).
