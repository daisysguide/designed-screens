Behavior notes
All 7 categories present. Default: first category with actionable topics expanded, rest collapsed.
Tapping a category header toggles expand/collapse with chevron animation.
Sort priority within each group: Your turn → Not started → Waiting → Needs work → We're good → Locked.
Locked topics: no tap, no navigation. Visually de-emphasized at ~55% opacity. Applies to prediction cards only.
"Manage topics" link always visible top right — never scrolls away.
Topic count in subtitle ("45 topics · 10 complete") updates live as topics are completed.
Category names in Title Case verbatim — same as throughout the app.
