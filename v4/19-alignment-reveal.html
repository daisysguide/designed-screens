Three alignment states
🎉 Fully aligned
Both partners' answers identical or near-identical across all questions. Confetti animation. Green pill.
😌 Mostly aligned
Answers compatible but not identical. No animation. Yellow pill.
🤔 Worth a conversation
Answers diverge meaningfully on ≥1 question. No animation. Orange pill.
Alignment is evaluated across all questions — not per question. One pill summarizes the whole topic. Emoji is load-bearing — sentence case only.
