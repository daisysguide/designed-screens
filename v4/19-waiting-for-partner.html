Behavior notes
Push notification auto-fires to partner the moment this screen loads. Poke button is a second, manual nudge.
Poke limited to once per hour per topic to avoid spamming the partner.
When partner completes their answers, the app sends a push to the user and navigates them to Screen 20 Alignment Reveal — even if they've left this screen and gone to Home.
"Back to home" → Screen 11 Home. User will receive a push notification when partner is done.
No bottom nav on this screen.
Edits allowed until partner submits. Once partner submits, the topic transitions to "Ready to reveal" and the edit affordance is removed. If user is mid-edit when partner submits, the user finishes their edit; partner's submit queues; reveal triggers only when both have a fresh submission.
