Re-answer round mechanics
Initiated byEither partner can tap "Update our answers" on Screen 23
NotificationPartner gets a push: "[name] wants to re-answer [topic]. Want to re-answer together?"
Race conditionIf both initiate simultaneously, timestamp wins. Second tap is reinterpreted as joining the initiated round.
Topic state during roundInitiator's old reveal remains canonical until both have re-answered
Expiry14 days. If partner doesn't re-answer within 14 days, the round expires and the topic returns to its prior Completed state. Initiator's pending re-answers are discarded.
ReminderAt day 7, partner gets a gentle nudge push. At day 13, a final reminder.
Behavior notes
Either partner can initiate re-answering. The other is notified immediately when "Yes, let's re-answer" is tapped.
Previous answers are retained server-side but not surfaced in the UI for V1.
After both re-answer, the reveal (Screen 20) plays again and post-reveal reflection (Screen 21) resets.
"Nevermind" returns to Screen 23 Completed Topic with no changes.
No bottom nav on this screen.
V1.1 will offer the initiator a choice when starting: "update just your answers" vs "start the discussion over." V1 only supports the paired round.
