Appears in the Topic List as a visually distinct card. After answering → Screen 19 Waiting for Partner (same flow as regular topics). At the milestone date, app sends a push and navigates to Screen 20 Alignment Reveal. No bottom nav.
Three screen states
AnsweringNormal question card + options. Prediction header: dashed border, cream-tinted bg. CTA: "Lock in my prediction →" — disabled until option selected.
LockedQuestion card replaced by sealed card (🔒 icon, "Your prediction is sealed." heading, body + reveal date pill). Prediction header: solid border strengthened slightly, subdued bg. CTA becomes "Back to topics" — enabled. No countdown UI — reveal date pill communicates timeline.
Reveal readySealed card shows 🔮 icon, "Time to find out." heading, body confirming milestone hit. Prediction header: solid purple border + purple-tinted bg — signals the card is now active. CTA: "See your predictions →" — enabled, full weight.

Eligibility rule
Show only whenReveal date (due date + 3 months, or birth date + 3 months for "Baby is already here" users) is in the future

ExcludesTrying (or thinking about trying) (no milestone date) · Baby is here with baby > 3 months old (window has passed)
When user updates Q1Re-run eligibility on Q1 change. A "Trying (or thinking about trying)" user who transitions to Pregnant will see prediction cards appear in their plan on the re-filter.
Milestone timing
Reveal trigger3 months postpartum — auto-calculated from due date entered in onboarding quiz
Reminder pushSent to both partners when the reveal date is reached
Bridge to retention. This screen's purpose extends beyond the question — it creates a reason to return to the app months after onboarding.
Components on this screen
Prediction Card Header Question Card Answer Options (MC) Locked state card Button — Primary (lg) Screen Header (back)
Behavior notes
Dashed border on prediction header signals this is a special topic type — distinct from regular topics throughout the app.
Once locked, answers cannot be changed. The locked state is permanent until the reveal date.
Reveal date is auto-set from onboarding due date. Shown to user in the locked state.
At milestone: push notification → "It's time. See what you both predicted." → Screen 20 Alignment Reveal.
No bottom nav on this screen.
Prediction Cards are the only time-gated content in V1. The Locked state applies only to this topic type.
